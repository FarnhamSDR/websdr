Microwave WebSDR
This WebSDR is operated by the Southampton University Wireless Society in collaboration with Martin G8JNJ and Noel G8GTZ.

22nd June: 3cm, 23cm and 70cm dongles are now fitted with TCXOs. This should greatly increase frequency accuracy and stability on these bands.
20th June: SUWS WebSDR mentioned on ARRL News as one of the first receivers to pick up signals from the the newly launched cubesats.
30th March: Filtered Pre-amp added for 2m, has raised the noise floor but dramatically improved weak signal reception
